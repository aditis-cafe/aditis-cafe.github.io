Greet the Food

Popcorn Croissants

Small but mighty, these mini croissants are baked to golden perfection. They’re the perfect quick fix for that buttery, flaky craving – just the right amount to keep you coming back for more.

Cinnamon Rolls

Warm, gooey, and filled with love, these cinnamon rolls are all about comfort. Soft dough wrapped around a rich, spiced filling, finished with a glaze that melts in your mouth. A treat that feels like home.

Garlic Knots

Golden knots of dough, brushed with garlic butter and sprinkled with herbs. Savory, chewy, and utterly satisfying, these are the kind of knots you’ll want to untie with every bite – perfect for sharing, but you might want to keep them all to yourself.

Tadka Focaccia

Soft, airy focaccia with a bold twist – this isn’t your typical bread. Infused with the punch of a traditional tadka, it’s where Italian comfort meets Indian spice. Mustard seeds, curry leaves, and a hint of chili give it a flavor that stands out on any table.
