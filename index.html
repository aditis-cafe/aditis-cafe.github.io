Meet the Blends

Dibbi

Cardamom pods, cinnamon sticks, and coffee – slow-brewed to perfection. Inspired by the trusty masala dibbi – because sometimes, a little spice is just what the brew needs.

Nutraj

Cold Brew with a Filter Coffee twist, capturing the deep, nutty richness of South Indian brews. Full-bodied, bold, and just the right kind of satisfying.

Mandi

Fruity coffee with a light, crisp flavour. Perfect on its own or with a splash of peach syrup – because coffee can be a little fruity too.

Addha

Dark roast, light roast – a half-and-half blend that’s all about balance. It's for when you can’t choose just one.
